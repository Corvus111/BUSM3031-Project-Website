Welcome to my website acting as my report for my Business Leadership Influencial Leader Research Report!

My chosen leader to research is Gabe Newell, the CEO and Co-Founder of Valve Software. This website will go over who he is as a leader, some leadership traits, and some beliefs that are presented through his actions. Some examples of the topics are:

  • Not fearing to take risks on new products.
  • A near open source belief for software.

Naviage to each section through the navigation bar above to learn more about Gabe Newell as a leader and how his actions have and are affecting industry. I have also included a reflection of relating Newell's leadership trait's back to myself on what I could improve and believe to be important myself.
